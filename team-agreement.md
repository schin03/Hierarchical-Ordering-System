# Team Agreement

## Communication

**How will you communicate?** (e.g., Discord, MS Teams, texting, email)

- Discord, texting, and email

**How quickly will you respond to messages from your partner?** (e.g., within 24 hours, same day)

- Same day.

## Meetings

**Will you meet in person or remotely?**

- In person and remotely.

**How often will you meet each week (outside of the weekly TA meeting)?**

- 3 times a week

**How long will your meetings be?**

- 1-2 hours long.

**Additional clarifiers you may include:**

**How will you schedule meetings?**

- Schedule through google calendar.

**What happens if someone needs to miss or reschedule?**

- Making sure to notify the partner within 24 hours.

## Contribution and Workload Expectations

**What grade are you aiming for on the project?**

- A+

**What happens if teammates want different target grades?** (e.g., one aims for an A, one for a B)

- Never gonna happen. We are on the same page.

**Is it acceptable for one team member to do more work to achieve a higher grade?**

- No, we are trying to aim for 50/50.

**What factors might affect your ability to work on the project?** (e.g., other course loads, family obligations)

- Other course loads, family obligations, or emergencies.

**Which weeks will you have reduced capacity?** (e.g., midterm-heavy weeks)

- Final/midterm weeks.

## Implementation and Workflow

**Where will you record task responsibilities?** (e.g., GitHub issues, project board, shared doc)

- Shared google doc/ github issues and pull requests.

**How will you communicate deadlines for your tasks?**

- Through messages and in person

**What happens if you cannot meet a deadline you set?**

- Never gonna happen. But if it does we will try to finish it by tomorrow.

**How will you show your partner that your work is complete?** (e.g., PR description, demo during meeting)

- PR description, during meeting we will discuss what have we done and decide if the requirement is completed.

**Do you want to enable linting? If so, will you disable any default rules?**

- Yes, to prevent prettier issues.

**How often will you push changes to the repository?** (e.g., after each work session, daily)

- Every small change to the code.

**How long should code reviews take?** (e.g., within 48 hours of a pull request being submitted)

- Within 12 hours of a pull request being submitted.

**How large should pull requests be?** (e.g., one feature, <300 lines, etc.)

- Should be less than 100 lines of code.
